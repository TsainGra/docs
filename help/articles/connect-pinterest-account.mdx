## **Pinterest Board Setup**

Before linking your Pinterest account, you must have at least one Pinterest board set up. To do so, navigate to your Pinterest profile page and click on the " Create a board" option. Follow the instructions to create a new board.

![Pinterest Board Create Jp](/images/Pinterest-board-create.jpg)

## **Linking Pinterest**

### **Go to the Manage accounts page**

Open the manage accounts page from the social tab and click on the link button. This will open a new tab.

![Social Page Jp](/images/FB-article/social-page.jpg)

### **Click to Link Pinterest**

Click the Pinterest icon on the Social Accounts page.

![Pinterest Click To Link Jp](/images/Pinterest-click-to-link.jpg)

### **First Time Pinterest Login**

If you are not logged in yet, you’ll navigate to the Pinterest login page. Enter your credentials accordingly.\
If you are already logged in, please proceed to the next step.

![Pinterest Login Jp](/images/Pinterest-login.jpg)

### **Authorize App**

If already logged in, click **Give access** on the Authorize app page.

![Pinterest Approval Jp](/images/Pinterest-approval.jpg)

### **Choose a Pinterest Board**

Once back at the Ayrshare dashboard, choose the Pinterest board to link.

![Pinterest Board Select Jp](/images/Pinterest-board-select.jpg)

### **Confirmation**

Your Social Accounts page will now be updated with your Pinterest account.

![Pinterest Connected Jp](/images/Pinterest-connected.jpg)

## You're All Set\!

You’ve successfully linked your Pinterest board to our platform\! \
You can now **post or schedule media** directly through our dashboard with ease.\
Need more help? Feel free to reach out to our support team anytime.
