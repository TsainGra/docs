You can link your X, formerly known as Twitter, account with our platform. When authorizing X, you will be redirected to X to log in and authorize Ayrshare to access your X account.

1. **Go to the Manage accounts page**\
   Open the manage accounts page from the social tab and click on the link button. This will open a new tab.

   <img
     src="/images/Screenshot2025-07-04at1.54.16AM.png"

     title="Screenshot2025 07 04at1 54 16AM Pn"
     style={{ width:"70%" }}
   />
2. **Social Accounts Page** \
   Click **X/Twitter - Click to Link** button on the Social Accounts page.

   <img
     src="/images/x-twitter-click-to-link.webp"
     alt="X Twitter Click To Link Web"
     title="X Twitter Click To Link Web"
     style={{ width:"51%" }}
   />
3. **First Time X Linking**\
   When you click the X icon on the Social Accounts page, an X sign-in will pop-up - _please be sure you allow pop-ups_. If you are already logged in, please proceed to the following step.\
   Click _Sign In_, follow the instructions, and input relevant login credentials.

   <img
     src="/images/Screenshot2025-07-04at1.31.51AM.png"
     alt="Screenshot2025 07 04at1 31 51AM Pn"
     title="Screenshot2025 07 04at1 31 51AM Pn"
     style={{ width:"62%" }}
   />
4. **Authorize Access**\
   If you have already signed in, click **Authorize app** on the pop-up.\
   This will authorize Ayrshare to access your X account.

   <img
     src="/images/Screenshot2025-07-04at1.32.51AM.png"
     alt="Screenshot2025 07 04at1 32 51AM Pn"
     title="Screenshot2025 07 04at1 32 51AM Pn"
     style={{ width:"48%" }}
   />
5. **Confirmation**\
   Your Social Accounts page will now be updated with your X account.
