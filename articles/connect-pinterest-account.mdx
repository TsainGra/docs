### **Pinterest Board Setup**

Before linking your Pinterest account, you must have at least one Pinterest board set up. To do so, navigate to your Pinterest profile page and click on the " Create a board" option. Follow the instructions to create a new board.

### **Linking Pinterest**

1. **Go to the Manage accounts page**\
   Open the manage accounts page from the social tab and click on the link button. This will open a new tab.

   <img
     src="/images/Screenshot2025-07-04at1.54.16AM.png"
     alt="Screenshot2025 07 04at1 54 16AM Pn"
     title="Screenshot2025 07 04at1 54 16AM Pn"
     style={{ width:"64%" }}
   />
2. **Click to Link Pinterest**

   Click the Pinterest icon on the Social Accounts page.

   <img
     src="/images/Screenshot2025-07-03at2.40.53AM.png"
     alt="Screenshot2025 07 03at2 40 53AM Pn"
     title="Screenshot2025 07 03at2 40 53AM Pn"
     style={{ width:"37%" }}
   />
3. **First Time Pinterest Login**

   If you are not logged in yet, you’ll navigate to the Pinterest login page. Enter your credentials accordingly.\
   If you are already logged in, please proceed to the next step.

   <img
     src="/images/pinterest-login.webp"
     alt="Pinterest Login Web"
     title="Pinterest Login Web"
     style={{ width:"52%" }}
   />
4. **Authorize App**

   If already logged in, click **Give access** on the Authorize app page.

   <img
     src="/images/Screenshot2025-07-03at2.42.10AM.png"
     alt="Screenshot2025 07 03at2 42 10AM Pn"
     title="Screenshot2025 07 03at2 42 10AM Pn"
     style={{ width:"77%" }}
   />
5. **Choose a Pinterest Board**

   Once back at the Ayrshare dashboard, choose the Pinterest board to link.

   <img
     src="/images/Screenshot2025-07-03at2.45.14AM.png"
     alt="Screenshot2025 07 03at2 45 14AM Pn"
     title="Screenshot2025 07 03at2 45 14AM Pn"
     style={{ width:"84%" }}
   />
6. **Confirmation**

   Your Social Accounts page will now be updated with your Pinterest account.

   <img
     src="/images/Screenshot2025-07-03at2.45.53AM.png"
     alt="Screenshot2025 07 03at2 45 53AM Pn"
     title="Screenshot2025 07 03at2 45 53AM Pn"
     style={{ width:"77%" }}
   />

## You're All Set\!

You’ve successfully linked your Pinterest board to our platform\! \
You can now **post or schedule media** directly through our dashboard with ease.\
