---
title: "How do I connect my Facebook Page?"
description: "You can connect your Facebook page with these simple steps"
---

Only **Facebook Pages** are supported. Facebook does not support Personal Facebook accounts.

<Tip>
  Please be sure you are the admin of Facebook Page and **grant all permissions** to Pages and selections. Failure to do so might prevent successful posting.
</Tip>

1. **Click to Link Facebook**

   Click the Facebook icon on the Social Accounts page.

   <img
     src="/images/facebook-click-to-link.webp"
     alt="Facebook Click To Link Web"
     title="Facebook Click To Link Web"
     style={{ width:"76%" }}
   />
2. **First Time Facebook Linking**\
   A Facebook sign-in will pop-up - _please be sure you allow pop-ups_.\
   If you have already linked Facebook with Ayrshare, skip ahead to step 4.

   <img
     src="/images/image.png"
     alt="image.png"
     title="image.png"
     style={{ width:"56%" }}
   />
3. **Choose a Facebook Page**

   Follow the instructions and select a Facebook Page to link.

   <img
     src="/images/Screenshot2025-07-03at1.32.35AM.png"
     alt="Screenshot2025 07 03at1 32 35AM Pn"
     title="Screenshot2025 07 03at1 32 35AM Pn"
     style={{ width:"55%" }}
   />
4. **Continue**\
   Click **Continue as NAME** on the login page.
5. **Choose a Facebook Page**\
   Once back at the Ayrshare dashboard, choose a Facebook page to link and click **Submit**.

   <img
     src="/images/choose-fb-page.webp"
     alt="Choose Fb Page Web"
     title="Choose Fb Page Web"
     style={{ width:"70%" }}
   />
6. **Confirmation**\
   Your Social Accounts page will now be updated with your Facebook account.

   <img
     src="/images/facebook-linked.webp"
     alt="Facebook Linked Web"
     title="Facebook Linked Web"
     style={{ width:"70%" }}
   />
